Exhibition Center

Stanford 2015

An exhibition center designed for Jasper Ridge, near Palo Alto, CA. The building is designed with two wings, separated by an atrium space. It is characterized by its shading: horizontal shades shield the southern curtain walls from the summer sun, while in the glass atrium, operable overhead shades selectively let in sunlight when heating is required.
