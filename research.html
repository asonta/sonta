RESEARCH

As a PhD Student at Stanford University's Urban Informatics Lab, I use computational tools to study the complex relationship between people and the built environment. Within commercial buildings, I am working on understanding occupant behavioral dynamics and how people-centric design can improve not only energy efficiency, but also organizational goals such as productivity. On the urban scale, I am studying walkability, with the goal of making cities more livable and vibrant.
