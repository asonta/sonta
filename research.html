RESEARCH

My research is about the complex relationship between people and buildings. Spanning data science, building engineering/design, and social science, my work seeks to characterize patterns of behavior and the resulting design implications for buildings and urban spaces. I seek to develop tools that can help key decision makers produce spaces that are energy-efficient and that suit our human needs.
